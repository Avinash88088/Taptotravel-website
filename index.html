Frequently Asked Questions

How does NFC boarding work?

+

Simply tap your phone or NFC card on the device when boarding. The system instantly recognizes you and starts tracking your ride. No OTP, no waiting, no bargaining.

What if there's no internet connection?

+

TapToTravel works offline! All events are stored locally on the device and synced automatically when connectivity returns. You can complete your ride even without internet.

How is the fare calculated?

+

Fare is calculated in real-time using GPS data from the driver's device. It considers both distance traveled and time taken, ensuring fair and transparent pricing.

What payment methods are supported?

+

We support UPI, digital wallets, credit/debit cards, and cash. Auto-payment is available for seamless transactions.

Is my data safe?

+

Yes! All data is encrypted end-to-end. We use Firebase's secure infrastructure with 99.99% uptime. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

What is Shake-to-Activate Vira?

+

Shake-to-Activate is a unique feature that lets you summon VIRA AI by simply shaking your phone. It works even when the app is in background or screen is off. Smart pocket detection prevents accidental triggers when walking or in pocket.

Can I board without NFC?

+

Yes! If your phone doesn't have NFC, you can scan the driver's QR code to board instantly. You can also use physical RFID cards that can be registered in the app. Multiple boarding options ensure everyone can use TapToTravel.

Can I schedule rides in advance?

+

Yes! Use the "Schedule Later" feature to book rides in advance. Perfect for airport pickups, office commutes, or planned trips. You'll get notified when a driver is assigned to your scheduled ride.

What if I left something in the vehicle?

+

Use the Lost & Found feature in the app to report lost items. We connect you directly with the driver to help you retrieve your belongings. All reports are tracked until resolved.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does NFC boarding work?

+

Simply tap your phone or NFC card on the device when boarding. The system instantly recognizes you and starts tracking your ride. No OTP, no waiting, no bargaining.

What if there's no internet connection?

+

TapToTravel works offline! All events are stored locally on the device and synced automatically when connectivity returns. You can complete your ride even without internet.

How is the fare calculated?

+

Fare is calculated in real-time using GPS data from the driver's device. It considers both distance traveled and time taken, ensuring fair and transparent pricing.

What payment methods are supported?

+

We support UPI, digital wallets, credit/debit cards, and cash. Auto-payment is available for seamless transactions.

Is my data safe?

+

Yes! All data is encrypted end-to-end. We use Firebase's secure infrastructure with 99.99% uptime. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.
